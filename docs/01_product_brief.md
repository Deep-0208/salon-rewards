# Product Brief

## Product Name

Salon Rewards

Tagline:

Reward repeat customers in under 10 seconds.

---

## Problem

Small salons lose repeat customers because:

- No customer tracking
- No loyalty system
- No reward management
- No visit history
- Manual discounts

Most shops still operate using memory or notebooks.

---

## Vision

Build the simplest loyalty and rewards platform for local salons.

The system should become part of the daily workflow.

Owner should complete a customer visit in under 12 seconds.

---

## Target Users

Primary:
- Salon owners

Secondary:
- Receptionists

Market:
- Indian small salons

---

## Platform

PWA (Web Application)

Single active device.

Mobile-first.

Installable.

---
